About

Bio:

Hi there! As you may have noticed from my homepage, I go by the name Em and am currently pursuing a career in the fascinating web design and development field. When I'm not busy with my studies, I love to spend time outdoors - walking my furry companion, hiking, fly fishing, skiing, and occasionally, I venture out on backpacking trips. In addition to my outdoor adventures, I also enjoy crocheting during my free time. I find it to be a relaxing and creative outlet that allows me to unwind and express my artistic side. But it's not all about me - I also have a passion for giving back to my community. That's why I volunteer at local food pantries and food banks to help those in need. Additionally, I take pride in my commitment to continuous learning, and I find great joy in acquiring new skills, particularly in the domain of programming languages that I have yet to explore. My most recent pursuit of knowledge has been centered around artificial intelligence and its impact on the tech industry, both positively and negatively. I also have been diving into other computer science topics such as algorithms and computational thinking, object-oriented programming (OOP), functional programming, and boolean logic, among other things.
